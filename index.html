Control Your Pi-Hole
Anywhere

A modern, privacy-first client for managing your Pi-Hole server. Built with Flutter. Open-source. No tracking.

Features

Real-time Dashboard

Monitor queries, blocked domains, and activity instantly.

Query Logs

Inspect DNS queries with detailed filtering and status.

Blocking Control

Enable or disable blocking with a single tap.

Analytics

Visualize traffic, clients, and upstream performance.

Dark & Light Mode

Seamless UI that adapts to your preference.

Cross Platform

Android, iOS today. Web, Linux, Windows coming soon.

App Preview

Open Source & Privacy First

Pi Block is fully open-source and designed with privacy at its core. No analytics. No tracking. No data collection.

All data stays within your network and comes directly from your Pi-Hole instance.
