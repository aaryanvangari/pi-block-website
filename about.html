About the Developer

I'm Aaryan Vangari, the creator of Pi-Block. I'm a developer passionate about open-source software, privacy and self hosting ecosystem.

Pi-Block was born out of a personal need for a lightweight, modern way to manage Pi-hole instances without relying on a desktop browser. My focus is on creating tools that respect user privacy and provide a seamless experience for self-hosted services.

When I'm not building mobile apps, I blog at OpenSquares about tech, security, and my journey as a developer.
