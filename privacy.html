Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection: Pi-Block does not collect any personal data. All information provided (Pi-hole IP addresses and API keys) is stored locally on your device.

2. Third Parties: The app communicates directly with your Pi-hole instance. No third-party tracking or analytics SDKs are used.

3. Transparency: As an open-source project, you can verify how your data is handled by reviewing the source code on GitHub.

4. Local Storage: All app settings are cleared if you uninstall the application.
